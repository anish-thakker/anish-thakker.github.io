I Like Fridges

iOS app to manage your fridge based on photos of grocery receipts.

This hack was developed at Hackzurich. The idea was to process the receipts before feeding them to Google OCR API, then parse the results and store them in a data base. The app gave the user realtime informations about his fridge but he had to take stuff out manually :p. It was a cool proof of concept and I had the chance to try React Native for the first time. After this project I got really interested to ML problems and decided to learn Python as well ;)
